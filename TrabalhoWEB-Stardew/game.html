Stardew Valley is an endless RPG of country life! You inherited your grandfather's old farm in the Dew Valley. Equipped with second-hand tools and a few coins, you'll start your new life. Can you learn to live off the land and turn these absurdly vegetated fields into a thriving home? It won't be easy. Ever since Joja Corporation came to town, the old ways of life had almost disappeared. The Community Center, once the most visited place in the city, is now in ruins. But the valley seems full of opportunities. With a little dedication, you can be the person who restores the greatness of the Dew Valley!
